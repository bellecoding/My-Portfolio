About Me

My Story

I got into coding simply because I was curious. The idea that I could build anything hooked me immediately. I learned on my own for years before joining the SheCodes Bootcamp to pursue becoming a Full-Stack Developer.

What started as playful HTML/CSS experiments quickly became a real passion. Over time I dove deeper into JavaScript, Python, and eventually the fast-moving world of AI.

Today, I create AI-powered tools and smart web solutions that help businesses work smarter, from intelligent assistants to automation workflows and data pipelines.

My Philosophy

I care about clean architecture, clarity, speed, and building systems that genuinely help people. I love merging creativity with engineering to build tools that make a real difference.
